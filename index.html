Chocolate Milk

Whenever I'm asked what I'm passionate about, my mind immediatley goes blank, and then my mind ruminates on the exquisite nature of chocolate milk--how it pours, how it tastes, how it makes me feel... and after a few minutes I realize the inquirer has left me to my thoughts, without giving me a chance to explain. So, here I am. Welcome to my passion: choolate milk.

Fun Facts

  2. Chocolate milk tastes like a dessert without excessive sugar or add-ins

  3. Chocolate milk is inexpensive, making it the perfect recovery drink for an impoverished college student such as myself
